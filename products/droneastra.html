Non-kinetic counter-UAS, built for the field

DroneAstra launches from a compact ground module, autonomously detects rogue drones, and closes in to neutralize them with a net-capture mechanism. Captured aircraft are carried back for safe handover and evidence chain.

  • Autonomous launch, detect, approach and capture
  • Net-based, non-kinetic interception
  • Rugged airframe designed for repeat sorties

* Use subject to local laws and operational policies.

Detect, pursue, and net-capture

On detection, DroneAstra plans a closing trajectory, stabilizes near the target, and fires a net to entangle props and disable lift. The interceptor then secures the captured UAV for transport.

  • EO/RF-assisted target detection & tracking
  • Close-proximity holding & net deployment
  • Non-destructive, evidence-preserving neutralization

Safe return with the captured drone

After capture, the interceptor transitions to a controlled return profile. Load-aware control and safety envelopes ensure the aircraft brings the target back to ground crews without collateral damage.

  • Load-aware flight & descent profiles
  • Geofence, RTH and failsafes for urban ops
  • Secure handover at a designated drop zone
