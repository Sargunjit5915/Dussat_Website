Uninterrupted aerial overwatch

Patang is a vehicle-deployable tethered drone. Power and data flow through a smart tether, enabling truly continuous operations. Operators view the live feed, control zoom and ROI, and record footage from the ground unit inside the vehicle.

  • Drive–stop–fly in minutes
  • Continuous power & encrypted data over tether
  • All-weather design for long deployments

Unlimited endurance with smart tether

The vehicle-mounted power module feeds Patang through a lightweight, high-strength tether. Auto reel-in/out, fault protection, and wind-hold logic keep the aircraft stable and safe for long days and nights.

  • Continuous flight (tether-powered)
  • Automated reel management & failsafes
  • Stable hover in challenging winds

Long-range intelligence with 800× zoom

The integrated PTZ camera delivers 800× zoom for stand-off viewing. Capture 4K recordings and stream a clear 1080p (1k) feed to operators on the ground. ROI zoom and fine PTZ control make target identification effortless.

  • 4K recording • 1080p live stream
  • ROI zoom, precise PTZ control
  • Low-latency encrypted video

5G module for difficult coverage areas

Patang can carry a 5G backhaul module to extend connectivity where terrestrial coverage is scarce. Use the airborne link for command, streaming, and remote coordination.

  • 5G/LTE backhaul & local hotspot
  • Works alongside tether data link
  • Secure network transport end-to-end
