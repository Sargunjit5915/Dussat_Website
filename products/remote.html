Reliable control for real missions

Suryantra is a rugged RC platform built for field teams. It provides secure, low-latency control of airframes with long-range links, rich telemetry, sunlight-readable displays, and optional video downlink and AI-assisted flight modes.

  • 8 control channels with watchdog safety
  • AES-encrypted links: 868 MHz and 2.4 GHz options
  • Ergonomic joysticks, switches, potentiometers

Encrypted long-range radio

Choose 868 MHz (v1) for robust penetration, or 2.4 GHz (v2–v4) for higher throughput. All versions feature watchdog failsafe and AES encryption.

  • 868 MHz (v1): 2 km+ LOS, 8 channels, AES
  • 2.4 GHz (v2): 5 km+ LOS, telemetry capable
  • 2.4 GHz (v3/v4): 7 km+ LOS, video telemetry (v3+)

Telemetry, video & readable displays

Track vehicle status and mission data at a glance. Higher versions add crisp video downlink right on the controller, with larger TFT displays for situational awareness.

  • 5″ display (v2), 7″ display (v3/v4)
  • Telemetry from v2; 1080p video downlink from v3 (5 km+ LOS)
  • 5 navigation buttons, dual joysticks, 3-way/2-way switches, potentiometers

AI-assisted modes & 4G/5G streaming (v4)

Version 4 adds advanced flight assist modes and cellular backhaul. Stream video over 4G/5G, coordinate with remote teams, and keep control links resilient in tough RF environments.

  • AI-enabled flying modes
  • 4G/5G video streaming & remote coordination
  • Works alongside 2.4 GHz control/telemetry
