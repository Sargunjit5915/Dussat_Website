About us

We design and manufacture professional UAV platforms in India for logistics, search-and-rescue, persistent aerial security, and counter-UAS. Our engineering focuses on mission reliability, modularity, and field serviceability.

Dussat Global Technologies is propelled by the vision of seamlessly incorporating cutting-edge, perception-enabled deep technologies into the fabric of daily life, aspiring to create a world that is more secure, efficient, and environmentally sustainable. We believe that technology, when inspired by nature and designed to mimic it, can become a cornerstone of progress and well-being, enhancing the quality of life for the greater social good.

Our mission is to ascend as a global frontrunner in DeepTech innovations, driving transformative changes across industry, defense, and energy. By delivering groundbreaking solutions and leveraging advanced technologies, we aim to reshape these sectors—fostering innovation, operational excellence, and societal impact.

Dussat Global stands at the vanguard of the Perception-Enabled Technological revolution, combining expertise in Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, and Computer Vision with drones and robotics for defense, law enforcement, and the energy sector. We also pioneer research in Quantum Technologies and Post-Quantum Cryptography (PQC), setting new paradigms in secure communications and future-ready infrastructure.

Our guiding motivation is nature itself—understanding it, mimicking it, and innovating from it—so that every solution we deliver is resilient, adaptive, and aligned with sustainable development.
